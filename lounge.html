Lounge

When you need a place to study or get some work done for your next project, the lounge is for you. Even if you just need a place to get away from everyone and relax, play some games, and eat some food, then the lounge is for you. There are two areas of the lounge, the COSMiC zone and the quiet zone. The COSMiC zone has couches, TV’s, and video games for those who just want to relax. The quiet zone is for those who need to focus and get some work done. It offers state of the art sound proofing walls which blocks out any outside noise so you can have the most focus possible here. All people signed into any part of the lounge get discounts on food at the cafe.
