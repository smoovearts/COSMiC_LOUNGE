What is ?

Here at COSMiC LOUNGE, we strive to make this a welcoming environment for college students. We know how many students struggle with being away from home and we want to be that home away from home for them. Anyone, even if you’re not a college student, can still come on in and have the COSMiC LOUNGE experience who may want to play games, study, or just relax. College students recieve discounts on food and can hang in the lounge for free.
